Products

Solid Bricks

Solid bricks are modern building raw material. Manufactured using high grade OPC Cement, handpicked raw materials and by employing a modern yet robust processes and machines.

  • good durability
  • fire resistance
  • partial resistance to sound
  • thermal insulation
  • small dead load
Size (in inch) 10X5X3
Weight 4.2kg
Compressive strength 60-250 Kg/cm.Sq
Water absorption 5-12%
Density 1.5 gm/cc

Hollow Blocks

Hollow blocks are modular building raw material. Hollow blocks are hollow from the center making it comaritibly lighter regardless of its shape, size and dimention.
 

  • Rapid construction
  • increase in floor area
  • reduces cost
  • better insulation properties
  • Best for partitions
Size (in inch) 15X3X7
Weight 7kg
Compressive strength 60-250 Kg/cm.Sq
Water absorption 5-12%
Density 1.1 gm/cc

Light Bricks

These are light weight and have high strength to weight ratio. They are manufactured using flyash in addition to other raw materials. These are less porous and have less thermal conductivity.

  • Lighter in weight
  • Uniform in shape
  • Enviroment friendly
  • thermal insulation
  • High strength to weight ratio
Size (in inch) 9X4X3
Weight 2.8kg
Compressive strength 75 Kg/cm.Sq
Water absorption 8-12%
Density 1.7 gm/cc
